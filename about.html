ABOUT

Find out how education is one of the most important tools in shaping individuals lives and impacting communities!


WHAT WE DO

  • We try spread awareness on the positive impact education can have.

  • We try get people to come together and discuss different ways to make an impact.

  • Building a community where we can share knowledge and insights with the world.
WHY WE DO IT

  • We do it so people who don't have the resources to learn can be helped.

  • So people have a community where they can share their ideas on how to make an impact with education.

  • We believe that education people will raise the standards of soceity and help develop communities better.

HOW WE DO IT

There are many ways for us to contribute to spreading education all over the world. First of all we hold fundraisers to gather donations up to help provide resources to areas less fortunate than ours. This money will go to building schools, providing infrastructure, buying books and copies for students and paying teachers. We can also donate books and stationary items, as these will be collected together and dispersed fairly to the areas that need them the most. Latsly we can also make an impact in our area, we can volunteer at schools which are lacking facilities and help out for the day trying to teach students or even make a small impact in their lives.
